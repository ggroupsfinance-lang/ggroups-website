Payroll & Compliance Services in India

G Group's delivers professional payroll processing and statutory compliance management services across India. From salary calculations to Provident Fund (PF), Employee State Insurance (ESIC), and Professional Tax compliance — we ensure 100% accuracy, timely filings, and full labour law adherence with support from TDS compliance services and income tax return filings.

Compliance Reminder: Statutory contributions for PF (EPFO) and ESIC are generally due on or before the 15th of the following month. Professional Tax (PT) payment and return due dates vary by state, and our team ensures timely PT challan payments and filings as per the applicable state regulations.

Official Government Portals We Manage & File Through

G Group's directly handles payroll-related registrations, filings, and statutory submissions through official government portals across India.

EPFO Portal

Employer registration, monthly ECR uploads, PF contribution filings and UAN services under the Employees' Provident Fund Organisation.

ESIC Portal

ESIC registration, contribution filings, and employee benefit processing under the Employees' State Insurance Corporation.

Professional Tax Departments

State-wise Professional Tax registration, challan payments and statutory return submissions as per respective state regulations.

Income Tax Portal

Payroll-related TDS deductions, Form 16 generation and compliance filings through the Income Tax Department of India.

Frequently Asked Questions – Payroll & Compliance

What is included in payroll processing services?

Our payroll services include salary calculation, attendance-based processing, PF and ESIC contribution management, Professional Tax compliance, TDS deductions through our TDS filing services, and Form 16 generation aligned with income tax return filing.

Do you handle PF and ESIC filings for companies?
