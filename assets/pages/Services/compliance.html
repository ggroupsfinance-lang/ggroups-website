Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What are corporate compliance services?

Corporate compliance services include company registration, GST registration, labour law compliance, accounting, tax filings, and regulatory reporting. These services ensure your business operates legally under Indian laws.

How long does company registration take in India?

Private Limited and LLP registration typically takes 7–15 working days, depending on document verification and MCA approval timelines.

Is GST registration mandatory for all businesses?

GST registration is mandatory if your annual turnover exceeds the prescribed limit or if you engage in interstate trade, e-commerce, or certain notified services.

What labour law compliances are required for companies?

Depending on employee strength and state regulations, companies may need ESIC, PF, Professional Tax, and Shop & Establishment registration along with periodic statutory filings.

Can G Group's handle complete compliance for my business?

Yes. G Group's offers end-to-end corporate compliance management — from incorporation and licensing to ongoing filings and company closure.
