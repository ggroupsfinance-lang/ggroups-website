Key MCA & ROC Compliance Deadlines

Important statutory filings under MCA & ROC regulations with standard due dates.

Form Purpose Standard Due Date Status
MSME-1 Half-yearly return (Oct–Mar)
LLP Form 11 Annual Return for LLPs
DPT-3 Return of Deposits/Loans
DIR-3 KYC Director KYC (all DIN holders)
AOC-4 Financial Statements (30 days from AGM)
LLP Form 8 Statement of Accounts (LLPs)
MGT-7 / MGT-7A Annual Return (60 days from AGM)

These compliance filings are supported along with our income tax, GST, and TDS filing services.

Quick Compliance Tips:

  • Keep director DIN & KYC updated to avoid penalties.
  • File AOC-4 within 30 days of AGM to stay compliant.
  • Submit all LLP & company returns before due dates.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What are corporate compliance services?

Corporate compliance services include company registration, GST registration and filing, labour law and payroll compliance, accounting, income tax filings, and TDS compliance. We handle these processes to ensure your business operates legally under Indian laws.

How long does company registration take in India?

Private Limited and LLP registration typically takes 7–15 working days, depending on document verification and MCA approval timelines. You can start your registration through our company registration service.

Is GST registration mandatory for all businesses?

GST registration is mandatory if your annual turnover exceeds the prescribed limit. We handle complete registration and return filing under our GST services.

What labour law compliances are required for companies?

Depending on employee strength and state regulations, companies may require ESIC, PF, Professional Tax, and Shop & Establishment registration along with periodic filings. These are handled through our payroll and compliance services.
