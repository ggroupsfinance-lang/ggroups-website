Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for bank loan processing?

Banks typically require financial statements, GST returns (GSTR-3B), Income Tax returns (ITR), TDS filings, bank statements, and CA-certified financial reports. We prepare and structure all documents in a bank-ready format with complete reconciliation.

Do you prepare financial statements for SME loan applications?

Yes. We prepare structured balance sheets, profit & loss statements, and complete financial documentation required for SME and corporate loan applications. Our documentation is aligned with GST filings and Income Tax records to ensure accuracy and faster approval.

What is a CA certificate for bank loans?

A CA certificate is a certified financial document confirming turnover, net worth, or working capital position. These certificates are often required for loan approvals, tenders, and institutional funding. We provide complete support including financial preparation and certification coordination.

How does proper documentation improve loan approval timelines?

Structured and reconciled financial documentation reduces bank queries, prevents mismatches between GST returns, Income Tax filings, and bank records, and significantly speeds up verification and approval processes.

Do you provide documentation support for working capital and project finance?

Yes. We prepare complete documentation for working capital loans, term loans, and project finance applications including financial statements, GST compliance records, and Income Tax filings with structured presentation.
