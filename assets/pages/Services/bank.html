Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for bank loan processing?

Banks typically require financial statements, GST returns (GSTR-3B), Income Tax returns, bank statements, and CA-certified financial reports. We prepare and structure these documents in a bank-ready format.

Do you prepare financial statements for SME loan applications?

Yes. We prepare structured balance sheets, profit & loss statements, and supporting documentation required for SME and corporate loan applications across India.

What is a CA certificate for bank loans?

A CA certificate is a certified financial document confirming turnover, net worth, or working capital position. These certificates are often required for loan approvals, tenders, and institutional funding.

How does proper documentation improve loan approval timelines?

Structured and reconciled financial documentation reduces bank queries, prevents discrepancies between GST, IT and bank records, and speeds up verification and approval processes.

Do you provide documentation support for working capital and project finance?

Yes. We prepare documentation for working capital loans, term loans, and project finance applications with complete financial alignment and structured presentation.
